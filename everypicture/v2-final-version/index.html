    frog bucket hat

    This is one of the very first frog things I got. I went through a phase where I loved wearing bucket hats and this was my favorite one. My fashion has changed a lot and I don't wear it that much anymore, but it still has a special place in my heart for being one of the first frog things I could show off outside my house.

    frog heart keychain

    My partner is also a frog enjoyer so I gifted the other half of this keychain to him as a birthday present. The set contains two frog keychains holding half a magnetic heart so when they're together, they click into place and create a heart! Also, while mine is a little frog guy with wings, his frog has a little bow like cupid (which I think is adorable)!

    frog purse

    This here is Ricky the rain frog, a character from the Jellycat brand. One of my more costly purchases, I got this purse in San Francisco a while back before I even knew about the brand. I love Ricky because he can hold my wallet, keys, phone, and more-while looking comically grumpy. I don't know about spirit animals...but I resonate with Ricky. I have a miniature keychain of Ricky as well...but that's for another day.
    (Also I got the keychain from Ume Tea in Davis!!)
